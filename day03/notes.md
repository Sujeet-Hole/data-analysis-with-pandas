# Day 03 Notes

## What I Learned

Today I used Pandas to answer business questions from sales data.

### Topics Covered

* groupby()
* sum()
* sort_values()
* head()
* nunique()
* to_datetime()
* pivot_table()

### Questions Solved

1. Which country has the highest revenue?
2. Which products are sold the most?
3. Who is the most valuable customer?
4. Which month has the highest revenue?
5. How does revenue change month by month?
6. Which day of the week is the busiest?
7. UK revenue vs Rest of World revenue.
8. Revenue lost because of returns.
9. Unique customers in each country.
10. Country vs Month Revenue Pivot Table.

### New Things Learned

* Convert date using `pd.to_datetime()`
* Get month name using `.dt.month_name()`
* Get day name using `.dt.day_name()`
* Create pivot tables using `pd.pivot_table()`

## Summary

Today I learned how to use Pandas for business analysis. I used sales data to find revenue, customers, products, and monthly trends.
